  • Rohous Magazine

A trained architect, Daryl Landy's experience with renovating a small house in Baltimore led to the creation of ROHOUS Magazine. ROHOUS is a digital lifestyle magazine for maximizing life through small space living. Each issue will spotlight a different city, showcasing that city's most inspirational small homes with articles spotlighting a local restaurant, local merchant and a regional recipe optimized for small kitchens. ROHOUS Magazine will show you how to "Live Small, Live Grand"
